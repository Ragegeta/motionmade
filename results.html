Accounting Firm, Brisbane

Google Ads Management — Search Campaigns

The Challenge

A Brisbane-based accounting firm needed more client enquiries but had never run Google Ads before. They were relying entirely on word-of-mouth referrals and wanted a predictable way to generate new business.

14
New enquiries in 30 days
$107
Cost per lead
$1,500
Monthly ad spend

What We Did

Built targeted search campaigns around high-intent keywords like "accountant Brisbane," "tax return help Brisbane," and "small business accountant near me." Set up conversion tracking for phone calls and form submissions. Wrote ad copy focused on what matters to potential clients — experience, responsiveness, and clear pricing.

The Result

14 new client enquiries in the first 30 days from a $1,500 ad spend. The ROI paid for the ads and management fee multiple times over. Campaigns continue to improve month-over-month as we optimise.
